About us

Railead Co. with

Railead Co. offered a streetwear outfits such as t-shirt, shirts and merchandise. We always want to make sure our customer felt satisfied with our products. We always find the best material to be used in our shirts and merchandise to make sure our customer felt comfortable when wearing our products anywhere and everywhere.

Railead Co is based in Ampang, and we are still operational. When the founder's (Rakib Ansari) garments no longer fit the Malaysian environment, he was motivated to design his own. Railead is derived from the words "rail" and "lead." Railead, in other terms, is "a platform for being a leader."
